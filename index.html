Seeing Networks in Wellington City

We are always connected, everywhere and everday. Take a look around you, notice all those lights that are turned on or the cameras watching over you? They're all connected, tunneling under the city to one giant infrastructure.

The research below enforces this idea that everywhere you go, you're connected and everything around you is linked together.

Take a look at http://seeingnetworks.in/nyc/ to have a look at New Yorks infrastructure.













Household Items

These are common household items that help keep houses connected to the grid









Power Outlets

Power Outlets are found in every house and are a way that each home can stay connected to the grid.

Wifi Router

Wifi routers are found in almost every house and are a key way people stay connected to the world.

Sky Dish

Although many people have started removing these from their houses as they are slwoly becoming less commpon, this used to be such a common way households would stay connected and up to date with the world.

Automatic Door Sensor

First invented in 1954 and since been upgraded over the years, this automatic door sensor is a common item found at the store front of many shops. This creates a convinient shopping experience for all consumers. It is a metophorical gateway to show how connected each shop really is.
